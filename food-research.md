## Typical Refuge Hours (TMB summer season)
- Breakfast: 6:30-8:00 AM
- Lunch service: 11:30 AM - 2:30 PM (varies by refuge)
- Dinner: 7:00-8:00 PM (guests only typically)
- Drinks/pastries: available most of the day when open
- Rifugio Bonatti restaurant: 12:00-14:00, open 7 days/week in summer
- Le Relais Montagnard pizza: from 6:00 PM (pre-order early, sells out)
- Refuge de la Balme lunch: 12:00-2:30 PM
- Rifugio Elisabetta lunch: opens 11:30 AM
- Maison Vieille: may not serve food outside hours, beer available

## Key Findings
- Most refuges serve lunch 11:30-2:30, drinks/pastries available outside those hours
- Cash-only: Refuge de Miage, Chambres du Soleil, Refuge des Mottets, Cabane du Combal, Refuge Bertone, Refuge La Peule, Mont Fromage, La Kabana Crêpes, Alpage de Bovine, Refuge du Lac Blanc
- Card accepted: Col de Voza, Rifugio Bonatti, Chalet Val Ferret, Rifugio Elena, Courmayeur, La Fouly Supermarket, Col de la Forclaz, La Bergerie du Plan Praz, Le Brévent Panoramic
- Both: Le Relais Montagnard, Refuge du Col de Balme

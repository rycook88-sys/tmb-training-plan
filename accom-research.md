## Gîte Alpage de La Peule (Night of Jul 30)
- Type: Gîte (working farm)
- Location: 2106m, Swiss Val Ferret, base of Grand Col Ferret
- 33 beds, dormitories only
- Tel: +41 (0)27 783 10 41
- Email: nicolas.lapeule@gmail.com
- Reviews: "Family owned and special experience; staff was lovely and the fondue was to die for"
- Amenities: 2 showers, 2 toilets (outside), USB charging in dining hall, woodstove
- Food: Fresh salad, ham & cheese croquet on homemade bread, amazing fruit salad. Buffet breakfast with homemade yogurt.
- Packed lunch: 11 EUR (ham & cheese sandwich, fruit, snacks)
- Tip: CASH ONLY (Euro accepted). 4am cow milking may disturb sleep. Order packed lunch on arrival. Time bathroom visits before breakfast rush. Fondue is legendary.
